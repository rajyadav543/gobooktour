Luxury Rajasthan Tour with Tent Birding

Overview

Start and end in New Delhi! With the In-depth Cultural tour Luxury Rajasthan Tour With Tent Birding, you have a 10 days tour package taking you through New Delhi, India and 5 other destinations in India. Luxury Rajasthan Tour With Tent Birding includes accommodation in a hotel as well as an expert guide, meals, transport and more.

Tour Highlights

  • Experience Delhi, the National Capital of India, in existence since the 6th century BC.
  • In Jaipur, visit Amer Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal, and the local markets.
  • Enjoy the birding experience at RAAS Chhatrasagar accompanied by one of the knowledgeable naturalists.
  • Visit Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur, one of the largest forts in India, built around 1459.
  • Visit Udaipur, the most romantic city of India, famous for its lavish royal residences and artificial lakes.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in New Delhi

Upon your arrival at the airport in New Delhi, you will be warmly welcomed by our representative, and he will ensure a smooth transfer to your hotel. Take this day to rest and unwind after your journey, allowing yourself to acclimate to the vibrant atmosphere of the city. The rest of the day is at your leisure, providing an opportunity to explore the surroundings, relax at the hotel, or immerse yourself in the local culture. Overnight, enjoy a comfortable stay at the hotel, rejuvenating yourself for the exciting adventures that lie ahead in the coming days.

Day 2: At Dehli | Old Delhi & New Delhi Sightseeing

Today, proceed for a full-day sightseeing of Delhi. The National Capital of India, Delhi has been in existence since the 6th century BC. Start your sightseeing from the Rajghat, the memorial where Mahatma Gandhi was cremated in 1948. Later, visit the Jama Masjid, the largest mosque in India, commissioned by Emperor Shah Jahan. Later, enjoy a rickshaw ride in Chandni Chowk, the busiest market in Old Delhi. Afterwards, drive past President House, and India Gate in Edwin Lutyen's Delhi. Thereafter, visit Humayun's Tomb, built in 1562, commissioned by Humayun's wife Hamida Banu Begum. Later in the evening visit the Qutab Minar, the tallest brick minaret in the world, commissioned by Qutbuddin Aibak. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: New Delhi - Jaipur (275 kms/5.30 hrs drive)

After breakfast at the hotel, leave for Jaipur. Upon arrival at Jaipur, check-in at your hotel. Rest of the day free for leisure, later in the evening take a stroll inside the beautiful market of the historic walled pink city of Jaipur, where the old ways of life continue uninterrupted. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: At Jaipur

Today, embark on a full day guided tour of Jaipur. Start you sightseeing with the iconic attraction of Rajasthan "Amber Fort and Palace", A UNESCO World Heritage Site, built by Raja Man Singh in 1592 in red sandstone and marble. The magnificent fort comprises an extensive palace complex, divided into several sections. It stands on top of an easily scalable mountain, which is situated right next to the beautiful Maota Lake. The fort's majestic appearance and its geographical advantages make it a special place to visit. After Amber Fort, visit Jal Mahal (Water Palace), City Palace, Jantar Mantar, The Hawa Mahal (Palace of winds), Albert Hall Museum, and Birla Mandir. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Jaipur - Chhatrasagar (235 kms/4 hrs drive)

After breakfast leave for Chhatrasagar. Upon arrival check-in at the luxury camp, later head-out to enjoy the birding experience accompanied by one of the knowledgeable naturalists. RAAS Chhatrasagar has a very diverse bird life around, comprising of over 200 species. And the guests can enjoy bird life from close quarters almost everywhere in the camp. From inquisitive green bee-eaters and iridescent blue kingfishers to flocks of shoveler ducks, the camp life is nothing short of magic for birding enthusiasts. Later explore the farm, local temples and meet the villagers. Take a gentle dawn stroll around the lake, or an evening jeep drive through the grasslands, spying myriad bird species and herds of blue bull. Spend rest of the day, while relaxing in the lovely tented sitting room, and enjoying animated chats around a blazing fire under a canopy of stars, before settling into a culinary feast of a dinner. Overnight stay at the luxury camp.

Day 6: Chhatrasagar - Jodhpur (115 k,s/2.30 hrs drive)

After breakfast at the camp, leave for Jodhpur. Upon arrival check-in at the hotel. Relax at the hotel, later visit Massive Mehrangarh Fort - founded in 1459 by Rao Jodha, Mehrangarh Fort is perched on a 150 m high hill its sprawl is the most formidable and magnificent fort in Rajasthan. There are seven gates, which include Jayapol (Victory Gate) built by Maharaja Man Singh to commemorate his victories over Jaipur and Bikaner armies. You can still see the battle scars on the second gate of the fort. Also visit Jaswant Thada, the graceful marble cenotaph of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II. Later visit Mandore Garden, Clock Tower and Sardar Market. Next visit Umaid Bhawan Museum, built between 1928 and 1943, it's the world's largest private residence of royal family. Part of the palace is a luxury hotel, part of it royal quarters, and some part of the palace is dedicated as the Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7: Jodhpur - Ranakpur - Udaipur (250 kms/5 hrs drive)

Post breakfast, leave for Udaipur. On the way visit Ranakpur Jain Temples, built in 12 Century - popular for it's magnificent carving on 1444 non replicable marble pillars, each one has been designed to represents its own different theme. After sightseeing of Ranakpur, proceed to Udaipur for overnight stay.

Day 8: At Udaipur

After breakfast at the hotel, start your sightseeing tour of Udaipur with a visit to Sahelion-Ki-Bari. Then proceed to visit the Museum of folk art, which has a rich collection of folk dresses, puppets, dolls, masks, ornaments, folk musical instruments and paintings on display. Afterwards visit the City Palace, the biggest palace complex in India, where four generations of Maharajas added mansions and palaces. You also visit the museum of the palace, which includes the beautiful peacock mosaic and miniature wall paintings of Indian mythology. Also visit Pratap Smarak, Fateh Sagar Lake, Karni Mata Temple & ropeway. Later in the evening, enjoy a boat ride on the lake Pichola. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 9: Udaipur - New Delhi (By Flight)

Today morning as per your flight timing, our representative will transfer you to Udaipur Airport to board a flight to New Delhi. Upon arrival at New Delhi Airport, our local representative will meet you and transfer you to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 10: Departure from New Delhi

In the morning, GoBook Tours representative will transfer you to the New Delhi Airport for onward destination.
