10 Best Golden Triangle Tour Packages

One of the most popular tourist circuits "the Golden Triangle", covers three most vibrant cities in the India - Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. The cities are known for their rich cultural heritage, ancient history, and architectural marvels. The Golden Triangle tour packages offer a glimpse into the opulent past of India, and the best way to experience it is by choosing the right package. Here are the 10 best Golden Triangle tour packages to choose from:

1. Classic Golden Triangle Tour

The Classic Golden Triangle tour is a popular tourist package that covers Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. It offers a glimpse into India's rich history and cultural heritage. The tour begins in Delhi, where you can explore the bustling streets of Old Delhi. Next, you'll travel to Agra to see the magnificent Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Finally, the tour ends in Jaipur, where you'll visit the impressive Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and the City Palace. The Classic Golden Triangle tour is perfect for first-time visitors to India and those looking to experience the best of what the country has to offer.

2. Golden Triangle Tour with Ranthambore

Experience the best of India's culture and wildlife with the Golden Triangle Tour with Ranthambore. Explore the iconic monuments of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, and immerse yourself in the lush forests of Ranthambore. Visit the iconic Taj Mahal, the grand Amber Fort, and the historic Red Fort in Delhi. Spot the majestic tigers and other wildlife on an exciting jungle safari in Ranthambore. Discover the rich cultural heritage and vibrant atmosphere of these cities. Book your Golden Triangle Tour with Ranthambore National Park for an unforgettable journey.

3. Golden Triangle Tour with Varanasi

The Golden Triangle Tour with Varanasi, is an extended Golden Triangle tour which let you experience Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, along with Varanasi, one of the most sacred cities in India dating to the 11th century B.C. Varanasi is regarded as the spiritual capital of India.

4. Golden Triangle Tour with Pushkar

This tour package combines the Golden Triangle with a visit to Pushkar, a holy town known for its Brahma Temple and Pushkar Lake. The package includes visits to popular tourist destinations in Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, and Pushkar.

5. Golden Triangle Tour with Udaipur

The Golden Triangle with Udaipur tour package is a 8-day trip that covers the cities of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, and Udaipur. This tour offers a unique blend of the famous Golden Triangle and the charming city of Udaipur, also known as the City of Lakes. The tour includes visits to some of the most iconic landmarks, such as the Taj Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace, and also offers a chance to explore Udaipur's stunning lakes, temples, and palaces. The tour is perfect for those who want to experience the best of both worlds, the classic Golden Triangle and the serene beauty of Udaipur.

6. Golden Triangle Tour with Goa

The 10-day Golden Triangle with Goa tour is an exciting trip that covers the cities of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, and Goa. This tour offers a unique combination of the famous Golden Triangle and a relaxing beach holiday in Goa. The tour includes visits to some of the most iconic landmarks, such as the Taj Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace, and also offers a chance to explore Goa's beautiful beaches, water sports, and local cuisine. The tour is perfect for those who want to experience the best of both worlds, the classic Golden Triangle and a rejuvenating beach vacation.

7. Golden Triangle Tour Khajuraho and Varanasi

The 10-day Classical India Tour is an exciting trip that covers the cities of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, Khajuraho, Orchha and Varanasi. This tour offers an opportunity to explore the cultural heritage, historical monuments, and spiritual traditions of India. From the stunning Taj Mahal and the majestic forts and palaces of Jaipur, to the ancient temples of Khajuraho and the spiritual rituals of Varanasi, this tour provides an unforgettable and immersive experience of India's diverse and vibrant culture. With the addition of Orchha, the tour offers a chance to visit a lesser-known gem with a rich history and architecture.

8. Taj Temples and Tigers India Tour

The 10-day Taj Temples and Tigers India Tour is an extensive trip that covers multiple cities, national parks, and landmarks in India. The tour takes you on a journey to explore New Delhi, Agra, Fatehpur Sikri, Ranthambore National Park, Jaipur, Varanasi, Sarnath, and Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary. This tour offers a unique combination of historical landmarks, cultural richness, and wildlife sightings. The tour includes visits to some of the most iconic landmarks, such as the Taj Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace, and also offers a chance to explore national parks like Ranthambore to spot tigers, and bird sanctuaries like Bharatpur to see a wide variety of birds. The tour is perfect for those who want to experience the best of India's rich heritage and biodiversity.

9. Golden Triangle Tour with Rajasthan

The Golden Triangle Tour with Rajasthan, is an extended Golden Triangle tour which let you experience Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, along with Rajasthan. The land of the Kings & Emperors Rajasthan, is the birthplace of many brave warriors, whose heroic tales are sung till now. If you were willing to know India closely then you should visit Rajasthan.

10. Golden Triangle Tour with Nepal

India's Golden Triangle Tour with Nepal is an exciting trip that covers the cities of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, and also the beautiful country of Nepal. This tour offers a unique combination of the famous Golden Triangle in India and the cultural richness of Nepal. The tour is perfect for those who want to experience the best of both countries India & Nepal.
